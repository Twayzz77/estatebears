Frequently Asked Questions

How does the property purchase process work? +

The process includes consultation, property selection, viewings, legal clearance verification, price negotiations, document preparation and transaction registration. This typically takes from 2 weeks to 3 months depending on your requirements and market conditions.

What commission do you charge? +

Our commission ranges from 2% to 5% depending on the selected service package and property value. The commission is only paid after successful completion of the transaction. See the "Pricing" section for details.

Do you provide legal guarantees? +

Yes, we provide legal guarantees for all transactions. Our legal team thoroughly checks each property, and we also provide title insurance in case of hidden legal issues that couldn't be identified during verification.

How quickly can you sell my property? +

On average, properties sell within 2-4 weeks given adequate pricing and quality presentation. In the "Premium" package, we provide a 30-day sale guarantee or refund the commission difference.

Do you work with mortgage clients? +

Yes, we actively work with mortgage clients. Our specialists will help select the optimal mortgage program, prepare documents for the bank, and support the transaction until you receive the keys. We have partnerships with leading banks in the country.
